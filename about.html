A Little About Me

My Story

Cat lover, art enthusiast, UI designer, small business owner, big thinker. I’m a junior UI designer based in fabulous Las Vegas, Nevada. I’m a thorough person who loves to obsess over the details in any given work of art, from UI design to miniature scale fashion. I grew up an avid artist and fashion designer, teaching myself to sew at age 11, knowing I was destined for a career centered on helping others and using design to do it! While I currently own a small business dedicated to creating high-end doll fashion for collectors globally, I’m seeking to move on to becoming a full-time junior UI designer remotely in the Las Vegas area.

I love being able to create beautiful, clean interfaces that help others achieve their goals! User satisfaction, from fashion to UI, is my utmost priority. Tackling a common user problem by troubleshooting new methods until something fits just right is one of my favorite things to do. I’m also motivated by strong communication between myself and my team- teamwork makes the dream work, right? Generally I don’t take myself too seriously and I like being able to ask for feedback from others to improve my work. I’m constantly evolving and growing to become a better artist in every form.

When I’m not crafting my next masterpiece, I can be found spending time with my family or friends in one way or another- from binging a TV show with my family or playing a round of Mario Party with my best friends. I love spending my summers in the mountains and taking walks to look at the sunset. I also like to cook, bake, and spend time with my two cats. I’d say my favorite activity, though, is going to local classic rock concerts and enjoying live entertainment. There’s nothing like experiencing the real thing, videos can’t do it justice!
