ArteClub Case Study

Arteclub is a mobile application for the creative person seeking local art classes and events. No more scouring the web for random events that are hard to keep straight! With Arteclub, events are categorized and grouped in such a way that any user can find a class they'd enjoy, book their spot, and view their booking in a localized calendar.
