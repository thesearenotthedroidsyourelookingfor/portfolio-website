Arteclub Case Study

Arteclub is an app I was responsible for ideating, researching, concepting, testing, wireframing, and designing. Essentially, I took on the full-time role of both UX and UI developer for ArteClub. It all started with an issue- the fact that artists don't have one single source to find local events. Using this, I brainstormed, designed, tested, and iterated on Arteclub until I came up with a finished product that could relieve many artists' frustration. Check out my entire process here!
